COVID-19 RESOLUTIONS

We are here for your, with you

Due to COVID-19, many individual's mental health and wellbeing have been affected where several people had to distance themselves from their loved ones which led them to isolation and this has negatively resulted in their mental health ad wellbeing.

Stress was a major factor that has been caused by COVID-19. Many individuals have taken action that caused them to lose their life, hope, love and family.

So, at LIFE, we are here to let you know that someone is always by your side who wishes to see a smile on your face without losing hope or having fear or any regrets.

Hence, to relieve your stress, depression and/or anxiety, LIFE believes that Yoga, Meditation and Stretching are the main components to improve an individual's mental health and wellbeing. It allows you to relax and control your mind leading you to gain peace from all stressful thoughts. The below activites allows you to improve your mental health and wellbeing and enjoy your life to the fullest.
